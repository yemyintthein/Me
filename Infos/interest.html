My Interests

Reading

Here are some of my favorite books:

Meditations by Marcus Aurelius

A series of personal writings by the Roman Emperor Marcus Aurelius, in which he reflects on his own life and the nature of existence, offering insights into Stoic philosophy.

Ethics by Aristotle

Aristotle's ethics, or study of character, is built around the premise that people should achieve an excellent character (a virtuous character, "ethikē aretē" in Greek) by practicing virtue in order to ultimately attain happiness or well-being (eudaimonia).

The Prince by Niccolò Machiavelli

A political treatise by the Italian Renaissance political theorist Niccolò Machiavelli, offering advice on how rulers can maintain power and control, often characterized by its pragmatic and sometimes ruthless approach.

Games

Red Dead Redemption 2

An action-adventure game set in the American Old West, developed by Rockstar Games. Follows outlaw Arthur Morgan's adventures and struggles in the dying Wild West.

Genshin Impact

An action role-playing game developed and published by miHoYo. Players control "Travelers" in the land of Teyvat, battling enemies, and uncovering secrets.

Albion Online

A sandbox MMORPG set in an open medieval fantasy world. Players can engage in PvP, crafting, trading, and more in a player-driven economy.

Anime

Chainsaw Man

A dark fantasy manga series written and illustrated by Tatsuki Fujimoto, following the story of Denji, a young man with a chainsaw for a head and a dog for a pet, as he becomes a Devil Hunter.

Jujutsu Kaisen

An anime adaptation of the manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami, following the story of Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes a part of the world of Jujutsu Sorcerers.

One Piece

A long-running manga and anime series created by Eiichiro Oda, following the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew in search of the One Piece treasure to become the Pirate King.

Favorite Animals

Arabian horse

The Arabian horse is a breed of horse that originated on the Arabian Peninsula. With a distinctive head shape and high tail carriage, Arabian horses are known for their endurance and stamina.

Peregrine falcon

The peregrine falcon is a bird of prey known for its speed and agility. It is one of the fastest animals in the world, capable of reaching speeds of over 320 km/h (200 mph) when diving to catch prey.

Sea Otter

The sea otter is a marine mammal native to the coasts of the northern and eastern North Pacific Ocean. They are known for their playful behavior and use of tools, such as rocks, to crack open shellfish.
